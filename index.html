PhD Thesis

2025 Dalarna University

Undernutrition and food security in children in internally displaced person camps: Prevalence, risk factors, and the impact of a cash transfer and nutrition counseling intervention

This dissertation examines undernutrition and food security among children in internally displaced person (IDP) camps in Somalia. The research combines prevalence studies, risk factor analysis, and intervention evaluation of cash transfers and nutrition counseling programs. The work provides evidence-based insights for improving nutritional outcomes in humanitarian settings and refugee populations.
